The honest bit

Why is it free?

No catch, just a simple trade: some people love the flowers and ask to keep them with one of our home plans. That's our whole business model — your gift is our introduction.

Our promises to you and your clients:
  • We always call first. Delivery is arranged around your client, never sprung on them.
  • Never a hard sell. The conversation is about your gift. If they ask about keeping the flowers, we'll happily tell them — and that's it.
  • Details stay private. Your client's information is used to deliver your gift and for nothing else. Never sold, never spammed.
  • No cost, no quota, no contract. Gift one settlement a year or every single one.

Selling and renting, residential and commercial — property managers and commercial leasing agents are very welcome. A new office fit-out deserves flowers too.

FAQ

Who pays for the flowers and delivery?
We do — Floral Image Canberra. The arrangement, the card, the phone call and the delivery are all on us. There is genuinely no invoice, ever.
How quickly is it delivered?
We deliver across Canberra on set run days each week — typically your client has flowers within a few business days of you logging the gift. Tell us the settlement or move-in date and we'll time it right.
What does my client actually receive?
A premium designer arrangement (around $100 retail) from our current range — life-like, allergy-free, zero maintenance — plus your printed card. Hand-delivered by our team.
What will you say when you call them?
You'll see the exact wording before you submit — and you can edit it. The default: "Hi, it's the team from Floral Image Canberra. [Your name] from [your agency] has arranged a welcome gift of designer flowers for your new home — we're just calling to find the best time to drop them off."
Can my whole office use it?
Yes — we'll set up a page for your agency with your logo, and every agent can gift from it under their own name. We can also set up individual agent pages on request.
Is there a limit?
We cap gifting at four per agent per month while the program grows, so every delivery stays personal. Need more? Talk to us.
